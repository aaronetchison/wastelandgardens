Chaos Symmetry

By: Aaron Etchison | Posted: 09/23/2011

This piece of art was done by Aaron Etchison for the song The Beginning. It is a digital design that was created with Photoshop.

The piece is meant to represent chaos that can come from new beginnings, but if you have an ordered mind, that chaos can be contained and controlled

Creativity

By: Aaron Etchison | Posted: 09/09/2011

I just want to talk a little bit about creativity.

True creativity is not something that can be manufactured or commanded at will. It is a very fickle thing that requires patience because it can come or go in a second.

We never know when a creative thought will strike us, so you should always be prepared with a pen and paper or voice recorder. Don't lose anything, because a little creative idea can blossom into something great down the line.

Come Together

By: Aaron Etchison | Posted: 09/04/2011

This design was created in 2009 by Aaron Etchison using only Adobe Illustrator. It isn't associated with any of the songs of Wasteland Gardens, but it does contain several meanings.

The grayscale look is meant to represent the idea that nothing in the world is as simple as black and white. There are various shades of every ideology and every individual.

The overall layout is meant to represent the fact that even with all of our differences, we can come together as one.
