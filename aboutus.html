FAQ

What is Wasteland Gardens?

In 2010, a group of artists and musicians came together to create a new independent rock and electronic music band.

From the beginning, they wanted to fuse their music, design, photography and video into one collective experience. That experience is called Wasteland Gardens.

The purpose of Wasteland Gardens is to entertain and inform people through various forms of art, as well as to open a dialogue about the world around us.
