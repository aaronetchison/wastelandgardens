Some information about this site.

Music

The heart of Wasteland Gardens is the music. It provides the beat from which everything else flows.

The type of music Wasteland Gardens makes is very hard to pin down because it varies dramatically, and includes alternative rock, electronica, trance, drum and bass, hip-hop, blues, reggae and world music.

Check out the Music page and get a taste for yourself.

Art

All of the art that is created by Wasteland Gardens is rooted in one particular song. It is meant to provide some visuals to accompany the audio.

The type of art Wasteland Gardens produces includes painting, photography, sculpture, digital design and about any other way you can create art.

Check out the Galleries page to see more.
